Jing Liu, Wei Zhu, Di Li, Xing Hu, Liang Song
April, 2023 SCIS
Domain Generalization with Semi-Supervised Learning for People-Centric Activity Recognition

The proposed DGSSL method effectively enhances people-centric activity recognition by addressing the domain shift problem in semi-supervised learning manner, with autoregressive discriminator for adversarial training and two reconstruction tasks, resulting in better performance and generalization than three state-of-the-art methods.

Distributional and Spatial-Temporal Robust Representation Learning for Transportation Activity Recognition

This paper presents a parallel model called DSTRR for Transportation Activity Recognition (TAR), which combines automatic learning of statistical, spatial, and temporal features as a robust representation specific to TAR.
